Information Theory Metrics

Understanding the Metrics:

  • Entropy: Measures average information content. Lower compressed entropy = more compression.
  • PSNR: Quality metric. Higher is better (typically >30 dB is good).
  • KL Divergence: Measures how compressed distribution differs from original.
  • Mutual Information: Shows how much information is preserved.
  • SSIM: Structural similarity. Values closer to 1 indicate better quality.
